About Me

My name is Jeremy Cariaga. I am 31 years old. I was born on August 7, 1985 in Anahiem, California. I went to Esperanza High School and graduated in 2004. After graduating from high school I enlisted in the United States Air Force. I spent 8 years in the Air Force as a military police officer. After two tours in Afghanistan and Iraq I was honorably discharged and attended Robert Morris Unviersity. I graduated from RMU with a BA in Accounting and Business Administration and am now looking to start a career as a front-end developer.
